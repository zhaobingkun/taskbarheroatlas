Task Bar Hero EXP farm: optimize the clear you can repeat.

The best EXP farm is normally the route you can complete quickly and consistently for your account—not the highest stage that succeeds once.

Build an EXP farm test in four steps

  1. Choose a stable candidate. Start from a stage you can already complete, rather than a hypothetical maximum.
  2. Run more than once. Record clear time, failures and attention needed across comparable attempts.
  3. Change one relevant input. Test a Rune route, role swap or gear choice against the same target.
  4. Keep the faster reliable loop. Re-test when a patch or progression change moves the wall.

What is confirmed, and what is not

The official Steam page confirms a progression structure with three Acts and four difficulty tiers. It does not, by itself, identify the best EXP stage for every player. A valid stage recommendation needs the current patch, access level, party, clear results and a clear definition of the reward being optimized.

EXP Farm FAQ

What is the best Task Bar Hero EXP farm?

There is no responsible one-line answer without your version, unlocked stages, build and completion rate. The best current farm is the fastest route you can finish reliably for the reward you need.

Should I always push the highest stage?

Only when it improves the repeatable result. If a higher stage adds failures or long clears, a lower stable route can produce better progress per session.
