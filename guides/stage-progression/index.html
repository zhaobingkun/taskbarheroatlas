PROGRESSION GUIDE

Task Bar Hero stage progression: solve the wall, then push.

Progress is clearer when every failure becomes a diagnosis. Separate wave speed, boss damage and survival before changing your entire route.

PUSH OR FARM?

Push if the next stage can become consistent after a focused change. Farm if the test is still unstable or if the needed upgrade comes from the current reliable loop.

Diagnose before changing everything

  1. Repeat the failing stage and note the exact loss point.
  2. Classify it as speed, single-target damage, or survival.
  3. Change one relevant skill, gear effect or Rune route.
  4. Re-test before declaring the stage solved.
