STARTER BUILD

Task Bar Hero beginner build: give the first team three jobs.

A stable early build is easier to improve when you can identify who handles routine clears, hard targets and the run’s failure point.

BUILD FIRST, RANK LATER

Use roles to solve the next stage. A generic tier label cannot replace the clear, single-target and survival jobs that make a beginner loop work.

Three beginner jobs

Clear engineKeeps repeated waves from becoming the time cost.
Hard-target answerAddresses the stage or boss check.
Stability slotStops the run from ending before it pays off.

Make the build coherent

Choose Runes, skills and gear that reinforce the job currently missing. After a clear becomes stable, reassess the next wall instead of keeping every early choice forever.
