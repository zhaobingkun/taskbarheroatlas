FARMING GUIDE

Task Bar Hero gold farm: choose the route you can repeat.

A gold route should be judged by stable earnings over time, including failed runs and attention cost—not by a single peak reward screenshot.

QUICK METHOD

Time several reliable runs, note the gold result and any failures, then compare the average against another accessible route.

Gold-farm test

  1. Pick a target route you can already complete.
  2. Run it under consistent build and gear conditions.
  3. Count time, completion and relevant rewards.
  4. Change one bottleneck only when it improves the loop.

Do not mix farm goals

An EXP route and a gold route may not be identical. Keep the target resource clear, then use Runes or build priorities to improve the repeatable clear.
