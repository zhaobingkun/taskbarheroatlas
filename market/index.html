Task Bar Hero market: keep the timestamp beside the price.

A market listing is a moment, not a permanent valuation. This desk separates official market-status announcements from live prices that require a timestamp and source.

OFFICIAL STATUS BOUNDARY

The developer stated that, after the market reopened, listing of the top three item grades was temporarily restricted while stability was reviewed. The roadmap said it planned to lift restrictions within July; that is a plan, not proof that the restrictions have already been removed.

Read a listing safely

  1. Record the exact item, time and listing source.
  2. Separate a listed price from a completed sale when that information exists.
  3. Check whether any current restriction affects the item grade.
  4. Do not treat a single screenshot or thin listing as a market value.
