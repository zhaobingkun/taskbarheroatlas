MARKET NOTES

Task Bar Hero market: keep the timestamp beside the price.

A market page needs a reliable source, the exact observed time, and context about how easily an item moves. Without that, a “live price” is not useful information.

DATA STATUS

Task Bar Hero Atlas does not currently publish real-time market prices. This page explains the evidence standard for a future tracker rather than presenting delayed values as live.

How to read a market signal

  1. Check the source and timestamp before comparing any figure.
  2. Separate a listed price from a completed trade where that information exists.
  3. Consider supply, demand and how quickly the item actually moves.
  4. Avoid making a decision from one snapshot or an unverified screenshot.

Market and progression

An item is only valuable to your account if it solves a present build or stage problem. Use Gear, Builds and Drop Rates before treating market attention as a recommendation.

Market FAQ

Does this site have a live Task Bar Hero market tracker?

Not yet. A tracker will be added only when it can show source, timestamp, update method and an appropriate warning for stale or thin data.
