Privacy at Task Bar Hero Atlas

This independent fan guide is designed to work without requiring player accounts or game-save uploads.

SUMMARY

The current site does not ask for names, email addresses, passwords, payment details or game-save files. Google Analytics 4 is used on the production site for aggregated usage measurement.

Information this site does not collect

Task Bar Hero Atlas does not operate user accounts, comments, uploads, payments or a newsletter in this release. Do not send credentials or game-save files through unofficial channels.

Analytics

This site uses Google Analytics 4 only on the canonical HTTPS production domain to understand aggregated page views and site performance. No analytics code runs on local previews.

Third-party services

The site loads fonts from Google Fonts and Google Analytics. External links may take you to websites with their own privacy practices; Task Bar Hero Atlas does not control those sites.

Changes and contact

This page will be updated if the site adds a contact form, accounts, cookies, advertising, payments or a new analytics provider. For now, the project remains an independent fan guide and is not affiliated with the game developer.
