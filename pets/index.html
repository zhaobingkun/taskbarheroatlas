PET REFERENCE

Task Bar Hero pets: choose for the role your run needs.

A pet should be compared against the same activity as the rest of the build. Future pet records will carry a version and evidence label before they receive a ranking.

SELECTION RULE

Pick the pet that strengthens the active job—farm speed, a single-target check, or survival—then test it on the stage where that problem exists.

What to compare

  • Effect type and how often it matters in the run.
  • Interaction with the build’s skills, gear and Runes.
  • Whether the benefit appears in farming, bossing or both.
  • Current-version availability and effect text.
