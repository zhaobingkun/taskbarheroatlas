Task Bar Hero Chest Timer

Set a simple in-tab countdown for the next chest window. It is a personal reminder—not a claim about a universal or official chest cooldown.

HOW IT WORKS

Choose a time, press Start, and keep this tab open. The timer runs locally in your browser and does not send notifications or collect your account data.

Use a timer without guessing the game data

Different chest types, events, versions, and personal routines may not share one schedule. The safest use is to enter the interval you want to track for your own session instead of treating this page as a source of official cooldown values.

What this first tool does and does not do

It doesRun a visible local countdown in the active tab.
It does notClaim verified chest schedules or push notifications.

Chest Timer FAQ

Does the timer keep running after I close the tab?

No. The first release is intentionally a lightweight in-tab timer. Persistent reminders can be added later only when the behavior is clearly explained.

Why are there 30 and 60 minute buttons?

They are fast personal presets, not verified statements about any specific in-game chest cooldown.
