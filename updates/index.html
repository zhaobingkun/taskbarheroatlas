What players should do after a save-related hotfix

  1. Confirm the in-game version. Check that the client is on the current update before comparing a route or reporting a result.
  2. Close the game normally. The developer's roadmap notes that cloud-saved data can fail to reflect when the game is force-closed or a connection ends unexpectedly.
  3. Keep evidence with a report. When a result looks wrong, preserve the version, time, stage and exact item or action instead of relying on memory.
  4. Re-test affected guidance. A bug fix alone is not a balance change; only update a build or route after its relevant behavior is checked.
