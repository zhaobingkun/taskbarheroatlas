RUNE ROUTE / BOSS DAMAGE

Task Bar Hero boss-damage Runes: target the check that stops you.

Use a single-target Rune route when a hard encounter, not routine waves, is the active limit on progression.

WHEN TO USE IT

Route toward boss damage only when the encounter is the real wall. Confirm that the test is repeatable before replacing a farm or survival priority.

Boss route method

  • Use the same target and build for each comparison.
  • Check damage timing and cooldown reliability, not just a peak result.
  • Record completion, time and any survival failure.
  • Review the route after gear or patch changes.
